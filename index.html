FAQs

What type of problems do I post?
Share problems about broken things. Whether in your job role, social life, academic trouble or elsewhere, someone feels your pain and is ready to help. Join forces and collaborate with your fellow engineers, friends, or peers. If the problem is real and worth solving it will become viral!
How would my problem be chosen by an entrepreneur?
If your problem is both real and worth solving it will go viral. Viral problems have the most visibility and are coveted by the various people who have ideas on how to solve them. Entrepreneurship is so much more than the idea. An entrepreneur needs help to discover what is out there and worth solving. You'll help her through this process so they can propose to create an awesome solution.
Who are these entrepreneurs offering solutions to my problems?
Entrepreneurs go through a rigorous application Transparency is essential We verify the following information and make it publicly available when entrepreneurs apply to solve problems:
  1. Address
  2. Credit verification
  3. employment history verification
  4. Online identity (facebook, twitter, linkedin)
  5. Recommendations
Can anyone become an entrepreneur?
Yes! As long as re committed to solving problems in unique ways, you can be an entrepreneur. To be chosen by Problupers you need to demonstrate value and help show that re the best woman or man for the job.
Where will my contribution go?
The prototype is already built, paid for and fully functional. Funding Problup will go towards hiring talent to build out the product, pay for hosting, and work towards building up Problup. Our funding goal is based on our burn rate. If we achieve our funding target, we will have funds for 3 months of further development.
How do I get equity in these companies?
This is the stealth part, but ll tell you as much as we can. Our prototype utilizes both Open source and proprietary technology. Our stealth technology gives unaccredited or accredited investors access to seed-stage funding. For those concerned with legal regulations, ve consulted lawyers and our technology is compliant with the Securities Act of 1933. Securities Act of 1933.

The prototype is a closed session that will last 2 weeks. Contact us if you're interested in joining.
I want to help! How?
First and foremost, please tweet us! If you would like to help support Problup's growth, feel free to Contact us. Please let us know how you would envision yourself contributing and your skillset and we will get back to you.
When do you expect the service to go live?
Our first closed prototype session is scheduled for October 1, 2013. We will be continuning prototype sessions in parallel with development of a beta product, scheduled to be realeased February 2014.

Boston, MA
How do entrepreneurs get funding and followers?
When problems are shared users can upvote, follow and contribute to problems. Once problems reach critical mass, entrepreneurs can apply their ideas to the problems. The winners are determined by "likes" and funds pledged. The team with the most "likes" and funds pledged wins the competition and keeps the funds to build out their idea. Those who liked the idea will also have the opportunity to keep in touch with the startup and continue to stay involved.
Why is this process better than what is currently available?
Saving the best question for last! Discussing problems you're having is extremely fragmented. Either you talk with peers about your problems or go online to seek out those who have the same problems. A savvy entrepreneur goes lengths to find people like you and sees if there is enough people like you to make it worth it to look for solutions. It's about time we all have that happen in one place. Imagine an enviornment where you can discuss serious issues with likeminded individuals and have entrepreneurs jump in to make your life easier.
