Problup Technology


Gamification UI as you navigate the world with your expert eye

Build a following and get recognized for your ability

Problup's technology leverages gamification techniques and a crowdsourcing methodology to identify and discuss today's biggest issues, problems and opportunities. Select your expertise as a hardware junkie, code ninja, market navigator, or sales wizard and discuss the world with your equals.


Community


Spark the debate by starting a problem, opportunity

Community votes up the most important topics

Discuss the opportunity or problem from your expert perspective

You're a techie because you LOVE to be one. Gather together around topics concerning your field and discuss topics from a perspective you care about. You don't have to be a Partner at a big consulting firm to be credible. Speak on what you're passionate about and let those in your followers determine what is important. If you consider yourself a marketer, have fun talking about and debating the market projections of mobile advertising. If you like to invest, get together and discuss the future of SOLOMO.

FAQs

What type of topics do I post?
Share topics that you're curious about. You sense that something is there but you know there are a million floating pieces that you can't seem to put together perfectly. Innovation begins when an opportunity is discovered or a solution to a tough problem comes about.
Is there a prototype? Is so, what is it?
Contact us if you want to join. The prototype is a closed session that will last ~2 weeks. The prototype phase consists of users collaborating on user-generated topics. Each class of expert will discuss the topic from their perspective and vote on everyone's answers. The end result in the prototype phase will consist of three topics fully concluded with expert input.
I want to donate. Where will my money go?
We only accept very small donations. The donations serve to cover costs of hosting the prototype, and crowdfunder. Your donation will also serve as an indication that this is an attractive project and will help persuade people to join Problup as a partner, investor or user.
When do you expect the service to go live?
Our first closed prototype session is scheduled for October 1, 2013. We will be contiuning prototype sessions in parallel with development of our beta product, scheduled to be realeased February 2014.
